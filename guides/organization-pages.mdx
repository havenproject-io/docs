# Buyer Organizations

Clicking on the name of the organization will take you to the Buyer Profile Page. Here, you can see all the opportunities and contacts associated with that Buyer. You can press `Join the interest list` button if you are interested in working with this Buyer.

The `Opportunities` tab shows the associated Contracts & Funding opportunities that the Organization has posted.

![](buyer-1.png)

*The* `Contacts` *tab shows the associated POCs from that Buying Organization*.
