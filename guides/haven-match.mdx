# Introduction

Haven Match is your first stop every day to discover opportunities curated specifically for your Business. This tailored list ensures you don't miss contracts that align with your capabilities and preferences.

If Haven Match shows no current opportunities, this is a signal to update your profile. Once your profile is up-to-date, move on to **Search Opportunities** to explore the broader range of available contracts.
